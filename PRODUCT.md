# leconsultantdigital.fr — Product Brief

## Register
Brand (landing / consulting portfolio)

## Users
- **Primary**: Entrepreneurs and SME owners in Rhône-Alpes, France
- **Secondary**: Marketing directors, training managers
- **Motivation**: Find a trusted, proven AI consultant who delivers concrete results (not dreams)

## Product Purpose
Consulting and training services in generative AI (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Make.com) + external marketing director + IA certification jury. Position as the #1 AI consultant in France for practical implementation.

## Brand Promise
"You use 10% of AI. I show you how to use 100%." — Concrete, not hype. Proven results (600+ trained, 5/5 120 certified reviews, 15+ years digital).

## Tone
- Expert but accessible (no jargon)
- Direct and honest ("du concret, pas du rêve")
- Action-oriented (always a clear next step)
- Empowering (this is doable, with guidance)

## Anti-References
- Generic "AI buzzwords" (avoid "transform your business", "revolutionize", "leverage synergies")
- Overly corporate or sterile tone
- Too trendy (avoid crypto/startup aesthetics)
- Vague case studies (must have numbers: "+300% leads", "€5M revenue")

## Strategic Principles
1. **Proof first**: Logo wall, review carousel, measurable stats visible above the fold
2. **Clarity**: 3 distinct services, each with specific outcome (not "everything")
3. **Trust signals**: Partner logos, certified Google reviews (5/5), bio + credentials
4. **Action bias**: Every section ends with "next step" (audit, booking, contact)
5. **SEO/GEO**: Valence + France-wide digital positioning visible (mentions of location, nationwide scope)

## Key Content
- **Headline**: "Vous utilisez 10% de l'IA. Je vous montre comment utiliser les 100%."
- **Sub**: "Je transforme votre métier avec l'IA. Pas du rêve. Du concret."
- **Services**: Formation IA | Directeur Marketing | Visibilité Locale
- **Proof**: 600 professionals trained | 5/5 on 120 certified reviews | 15+ years expertise

## Technical Context
- Next.js 14 + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS
- Vercel deployment (auto-deploy from GitHub)
- Mobile-first responsive design
- No CMS; content in code (data.ts)
- Core Web Vitals optimized (PageSpeed 85+)
